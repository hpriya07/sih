AI TUTOR

Explore our interactive modules designed to teach effective water conservation techniques. Children can apply these methods at home, document their efforts with photos or videos, and upload their submissions directly to the website. These uploads are reviewed, with points and rankings awarded based on the evaluation.

SCENARIO BASED LEARNING

Play games that simulate real-world water challenges, using logic and critical thinking to make impactful decisions. Children will engage in scenario-based learning games simulating real-life water challenges, such as droughts and floods. Through these games,they will make decisions on how to address these issues, enhancing their problem-solving skills and understanding of water management

juxtApose

Participants are paired with opponents and choose a topic related to water management. They are then asked questions based on learned modules and must identify the three incorrect options from multiple-choice answers. This process enhances decision-making skills by requiring careful analysis of all choices.

CARD HUSTLE

At the start of the game, the player using the white pieces selects a question of varying difficulty (HARD, MEDIUM, or LOW). The player must then identify the difficulty level of the question card they receive and strategically choose a question of similar difficulty to influence the outcome.

Real-Time Water Quality Monitoring

A key feature of our portal is its integration with the Central Ground Water Board (CGWB) database, offering real-time water quality data, including pH levels and sulfur content. With ARIMA model predictions, users can track and forecast water quality trends, enhancing their understanding and monitoring of local water conditions.
